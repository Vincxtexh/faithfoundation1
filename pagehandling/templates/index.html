Nasrapur Spiritual Camp

Nasrapur, this year (2022) will be conducted on 26th - 29th May.

Every year FAITH conducts Spiritual Camps in the village of Nasrapur twice a year. These camps last about 3 days long,
and for a small fee you can get to experience living and staying among the FAITH prayer partners. There will be food service and a rooms included. This is a fun place for children and a spiritually educational place for adults to help them grow in Christ and build their foundation stronger. On the last day we also conduct short skits, by dividing the youth into small groups and a small competition is created. The winner of this competition also receives prizes.
