About Us

FAITH - FORSAKING ALL I TAKE HIM

FAITH COMES BY HEARING & HEARING BY THE WORD OF GOD. - Rom. 10:17

Br Noel Vanhaltren formed FAITH group a purely evangelistic group & have been actively involved in ministry since 1985. The ministry was named FAITH abbreviation for "Forsaking All I Take Him". Br Noel, tours around various places in India and abroad to do the Lord's Ministry. He gets invited to numerous countries including USA, Canada, South America, Europe, the Middle East, South East Asia & China.

Our policy

We do not accept nor do we give money to do the Lord's Ministry.
Everybody should attend his/her own Church regularly.
We do not form any Church, nor plan to form a Church
Our ministry is to help Christians grow spiritually
We do not Baptize anyone
